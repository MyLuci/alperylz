Mobile App UI/UX

FoodieGo

A playful food delivery app that makes ordering feel like a treat.

Role

UI/UX Designer

Timeline

8 weeks

Platform

iOS & Android

Tools

Figma, Protopie

Overview

FoodieGo is a food delivery application designed to bring joy back to the ordering experience. The goal was to create an interface that feels less transactional and more like discovering your next favorite meal.

The design focuses on vibrant visuals, playful micro-interactions, and a streamlined ordering flow that reduces friction while maintaining delight.

The Challenge

Most food delivery apps feel utilitarian and cold. Users often experience decision fatigue when browsing through endless restaurant lists. The challenge was to create an experience that helps users discover food they'll love while keeping the process fun and engaging.

The Solution

I designed a card-based discovery system with personalized recommendations, playful animations for adding items to cart, and a "mood-based" browsing feature that suggests restaurants based on how the user is feeling.

The color palette uses warm, appetizing tones with pops of playful accent colors to create visual interest without overwhelming the food photography.

Results

The redesigned app saw a 40% increase in user engagement and a 25% improvement in order completion rates. Users particularly loved the mood-based browsing feature, with 60% of users trying it within their first week.
